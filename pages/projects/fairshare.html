Fairshare



Introduction

This app works a bit like Splitwise. One of my degree's projects.

It helps people to keep track of their expenses and debts with their friends.

The user can create a group and add their friends to it.

Then they can add expenses and the app will calculate how much each person owes to the others.

Technologies

This app was developed using:

  • Java
  • Sqlite
  • Springboot
